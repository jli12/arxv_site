About Me
__

I like to think of myself as an avid learner above all else. The two quotes above seem to encapsulate this disposition best, as even just overhearing a tidbit in passing can lead me down a rabbit-hole of readings from 20 parallel tabs, and bear fruit as a budding obsession.
I'm fascinated and utterly captivated by topics such as: the mechanisms underlying memory and consciousness, the root impulses behind how humans decide to act, emergent phenomena of microstates on the quantum level to the societal level, the way our intangible minds interface our flesh with this world and derive physicality when everything at the quantum level is ultimately a wave, and on and on. Through the use of computation and Machine Learning, I hope to be able to pursue these questions, as well as any and all I have and that may come up with in the future.

Outside of my academics, I enjoy staying active through swimming, running, and biking (though I wouldn't consider it triathlon training just yet) as well as bouldering. I also greatly enjoy baking-- sourdough breads especially-- and cooking in general, and maintain (to the best of my ability) this photography portfolio. Oh, and in my copious free time, I do very much enjoy ruminating over various ontological and epistemological questions as well :)
